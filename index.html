About

About
Tonda Tech Hub

Tonda Tech Hub is a youth-centered innovation hub focused on empowering young people through technology, digital skills, creativity, and innovation.

The hub was inspired by the belief that technology can create opportunities for young people even with limited resources. Through mentorship, practical training, and collaboration, Tonda Tech Hub helps transform ideas into impactful solutions and sustainable income opportunities.

Our Objectives
Digital Skills Training / Youth Empowerment / Innovation Support / Startup Incubation / Creative Technology / Community Outreach / Collaboration & Mentorship

Tonda Tech Hub aims to bridge the gap between young innovators and technology opportunities by providing an environment where creativity, learning, and problem-solving can grow together.
